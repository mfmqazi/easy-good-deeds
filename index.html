About the Book

"Easy Good Deeds" by Mufti Taqi Usmani is a valuable guide that details numerous simple actions a person can undertake to accrue abundant rewards. The book covers a wide array of topics, from worship to social conduct, emphasizing that these deeds require minimal effort but yield significant spiritual benefits.

This comprehensive work outlines 82 such deeds, making Islamic teachings accessible and encouraging regular, small acts of worship. The book aims to instill a spirit of practicing Islam in its entirety, showing how everyday actions can become acts of worship when done with the right intention.

About the Author

Mufti Muhammad Taqi Usmani is a renowned Islamic scholar, jurist, and author. He is an expert in Islamic jurisprudence (fiqh), economics, and hadith. His works are widely respected and have been translated into numerous languages, making Islamic knowledge accessible to millions worldwide.
